## Card Browser Configuration

### `mode`

Controls how the Card Viewer opens when you click **View** in the toolbar.

- **`"embedded"`** (default) — The viewer replaces Anki's main content area,
  appearing inline like the Decks or Review screens. Click any other toolbar
  button (Decks, Add, Browse, etc.) to leave the viewer.

- **`"window"`** — The viewer opens in a separate floating window, allowing
  you to use it side-by-side with Anki's main window.

Restart Anki after changing this setting.
